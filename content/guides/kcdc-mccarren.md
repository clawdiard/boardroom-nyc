McCarren Park's skatepark is a crown jewel of NYC's public skate infrastructure.

## Features

- **Bowl:** Deep, smooth, fast — one of the best in the city
- **Street Section:** Ledges, rails, manual pads, hubbas
- **Banks & Quarter Pipes:** Great flow options connecting sections
- **Flatground:** Ample space around the perimeter

## Tips

- Weekend mornings before 10 AM are golden — after that it's a zoo
- KCDC skate shop is nearby for supplies
- The bowl has a pecking order — wait your turn and you'll get runs
- Water fountain available in the park
