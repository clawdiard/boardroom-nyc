A bit off the beaten path, Owl's Head rewards the journey with great terrain and views.

## Features

- **Bowl:** Deep and well-shaped with coping
- **Snake Run:** Flowing banked run, rare for NYC parks
- **Ledges & Banks:** Solid street section
- **Views:** Overlooks the Narrows and harbor

## Tips

- R train to Bay Ridge Ave, then a short walk
- Bring water and snacks — limited options nearby
- Best vibes on weekday afternoons
- The snake run is unique to this park — don't miss it
