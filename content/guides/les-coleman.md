Located in the heart of the Lower East Side, Coleman Park's skatepark is a community favorite.

## Features

- **Mini Ramp:** Well-maintained halfpipe, great for learning transitions
- **Flatground:** Generous smooth area for flip tricks and games of SKATE
- **Ledges:** Low and mid-height, good wax
- **Rails:** A couple of rails at approachable heights

## Tips

- After-school hours (3–6 PM) get packed — go early or late
- Great food nearby on Rivington and Stanton streets
- Well-lit for evening sessions
