## The Queens Skate Scene

Queens might only have one dedicated shop right now, but don't sleep on the borough's skate culture:

- **Flushing Meadows Park** has smooth paths and open space for cruising
- **Rockaway Beach** brings a whole different skate vibe in summer
- **Astoria** has a growing street scene with DIY spots popping up
- **Jamaica Bay** area is underrated for longboard cruising

The borough is ripe for growth. As more riders realize you don't have to cross the East River for good skating, Queens will only get stronger.
