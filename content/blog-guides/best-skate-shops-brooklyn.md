## Tips for Visiting Brooklyn Skate Shops

- **Get there by train.** The L and M lines hit Bushwick; the J/M/Z get you to Williamsburg. Skip driving.
- **Combine with a session.** Brooklyn has incredible street spots and parks ‚Äî McCarren, Cooper Park, and the Bushwick DIY scene are all nearby.
- **Support local.** These shops survive on community support. Buy a deck, grab some hardware, or just say what's up.
- **Check hours.** Smaller shops keep irregular hours. Instagram is usually the most up-to-date source.
